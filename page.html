Bike Ride to the North Florida Fair

11/14/21, 6pm. We're meeting in front of Bicycle House (458 FAMU Way).
It's a 5mi bike ride on a smooth, flat trail. It's a very beginner-friendly ride.
Once we get to the fair, feel free to wander off! If you want to bike back together, we'll leave around 9pm.
Unfortunately, if it rains, the ride won't be rescheduled.
